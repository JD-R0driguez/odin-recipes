Cold Brew Bourbon

Oh, joy, another trendy drink: cold brew bourbon. Because regular old bourbon just
wasn't fancy enough, right? But hey, who are we to judge? It's just coffee and whiskey
mixed together, no big deal. Except, of course, for the fact that it's bold, refreshing,
and oh-so-smooth. Who needs boring old drinks when you can have a unique, complex
flavor that's sure to impress all your hipster friends?

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz bourbon (the good stuff, not the one you use for cleaning your tools)
  • 4 oz cold brew coffee. If it's not cold, it won't work. It's science.
  • 1 oz simple syrup
  • Ice cubes. If you don't have any, please don't attempt to make this cocktail. Stick to drinking water.)
  • Orange peel (optional, but it makes you look fancy)

Instructions:

  1. Grab a glass and fill it with ice cubes.
  2. Pour the bourbon over the ice. Don't be shy, give it a good pour. You deserve it.
  3. Add the cold brew coffee and simple syrup. Mix it well.
  4. If you have an orange peel, twist it and use it as a garnish. If not, use your imagination. A carrot peel, perhaps?
  5. Then, sit back, relax, and enjoy your delicious and refreshing cold brew bourbon. Cheers!
