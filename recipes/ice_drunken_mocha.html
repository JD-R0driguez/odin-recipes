- Ice Drunken Mocha -

ce Drunken Mocha: the love child of coffee and cocktails. Cold-brewed coffee, chocolate syrup, milk or cream, and a shot of booze, served over ice. It's the perfect drink for when you want to be a little bit naughty and a lot caffeinated.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of strong cold-brewed coffee
  • 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup
  • 1/2 cup of milk or cream (or whatever dairy substitute floats your goat)
  • 1 shot of your favorite liquor (we all need a little pick-me-up sometimes)
  • Ice (not the rapper, but actual frozen water)

Instructions:

  1. Wake up and smell the coffee, because it's time to brew some serious business.
  2. Add the chocolate syrup to the coffee and stir it like you're adding some spice to your life.
  3. Pour that delicious concoction over.
  4. Add milk or cream like you're adding a little bit of sweetness to your soul.
  5. Pour in that shot of liquor like there is no tomorrow (this drink won't judge you).
  6. Stir it once more and top it off with whipped cream and chocolate
  7. Let the flavors dance on your tongue like it's the last party you'll attend before becoming a full-time adult.
